EEG Mind-typing interface for LIS Patients

An Application to communicate and express their thoughts with the help of BCI Interface for paralyzed patients.At present, Locked-in syndrome patients are unable to communicate their needs due to complete body paralysis and speech paralysis. Currently, nurses and healthcare workers have to guess the needs of these patients. During my research work in NUS, I explored and worked on the development of a BCI interface to tap into the EEG signals of such patients to control a keyboard software in order for them to type out their intention using the OpenBCI headset. Processing and analysis of signals to map to specific thoughts like “up, down, left, right and enter” is implemented.

Versatile Remote Controller Mobile App

Android Mobile Application integrated with Internet of Things (IoT) to control electronic devices. The Transceiver is connected to the the device to transmit and receive IR signals. The mobile device is connected to the IoT device through sockets. The signal command is sent to the device from the mobile app which acts as a remote controller to control devices like TV, Air-con, Music Players, etc.

Word Castle is a brain teasing dual mode game inspired by the paper and pencil game "Bulls and Cows". This is the second game we developed in Route 4 Groups followed by Movie Maniac.
